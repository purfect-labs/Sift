# JobDash Product Demo — Script

**Duration:** ~36 seconds  
**Archetype:** Sage (authoritative, calm, methodical)  
**Platform:** Social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts)

---

## Beat 1: Hook (0-4s)
*Dark screen with subtle grain. Text fades in center.*

**Narration:** "Job hunting is chaos. We fixed that."

## Beat 2: What It Is (4-9s)
*Dashboard screenshot fades in behind text overlay.*

**Narration:** "JobDash is an AI-powered job search and application tracker. Your resume, your pipeline, your data — all local."

*Overlay text: "AI-Powered • 100% Local • Open Source"*

## Beat 3: Scraper (9-15s)
*Scraper screenshot with console log visible.*

**Narration:** "Upload your resume. Hermes AI extracts keywords. Then Google Jobs scraping finds matches — scored and ranked against your skills."

*Overlay text: "Resume → Keywords → Google Jobs → Match Scores"*

## Beat 4: Pipeline (15-21s)
*Jobs dashboard screenshot with pipeline columns.*

**Narration:** "Track every application through your pipeline. New to saved to applied to offer. Filter remote. Analyze gaps. No spreadsheet needed."

*Overlay text: "Pipeline: New → Saved → Applied → Interviewing → Offer"*

## Beat 5: Privacy + CTA (21-28s)
*Settings screenshot with privacy emphasis.*

**Narration:** "Open source. SQLite on your machine. No cloud accounts. Your job search stays private."

*Overlay text: "github.com/purfect-labs/jobdash"*

## Beat 6: Logo Outro (28-32s)
*Dark screen. JobDash logo + URL.*

**Narration:** "JobDash. Download it tonight."
